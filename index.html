Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Mission

"To protect and preserve the natural environment of Mt. Isarog and surrounding communities through proactive environmental stewardship, emergency response readiness, and community education — empowering every volunteer to be a guardian of nature."

Vision

"A resilient Bicol Region where communities, ecosystems, and wildlife thrive in harmony — sustained by a well-trained, values-driven network of environmental emergency responders committed to the protection of Mt. Isarog and its biodiversity."

Who We Are

About IVEERS

The Isarog Volunteer Environmental Emergency Response Service (IVEERS) is a non-government volunteer organization based in Naga City, Camarines Sur, dedicated to the protection of Mt. Isarog and the broader Bicol environment.

Founded by a core group of outdoor enthusiasts, DENR advocates, and community responders, IVEERS bridges the gap between environmental conservation and disaster preparedness — mobilizing trained volunteers for everything from typhoon rescue to biodiversity monitoring.

Our members come from all walks of life, united by a shared love for nature and a deep sense of duty to the people and ecosystems of Camarines Sur.
